About Me

Welcome to my page! I'm Ben, I have been in web development for 10 years, building career websites from scratch for hundreds of customers across a wide range of industries. While developers often devote their time to programming, I was fortunate to be responsible for both creative-control of my projects, and customer interactions.

Before switching careers into programming, I spent 5 years in recruitment and learned how to communicate with leaders in various departments at Salesforce. Today, I am equally comfortable as the main-point of contact for my customers' projects, whether I am communicating with Sales, Customer Success, Engineering, or C-Suite.
